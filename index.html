    Performance you can see and feel.

    Get adaptive performance and built-in visuals for a visibly better PC experience you can see and feel. 2nd generation Intel® Core™ processors — they’re visibly smart.

    • Intel® Turbo Boost Technology 2.0

      Intel Turbo Boost Technology 2.0 automatically speeds up your processor when you need it. So even if you’re running multiple applications at the same time, Turbo Boost taps any unused power to give you an even greater boost of speed. Like a modern sports car that delivers power and traction to the wheels where they’re needed, improved Intel® Turbo Boost Technology 2.0 automatically speeds up the processor when the PC needs extra performance.

    • Intel® Hyper-Threading Technology

      Intel® Hyper-Threading Technology lets your processor do two things at the same time, similar to a toaster that has two slots, instead of one. The result is that you can do a lot more in less time.

    • Intel® Built-in Visuals

      Intel® Built-in Visuals seamlessly deliver everything you need to enjoy sharper images, richer color and lifelike audio and video — so what you see looks amazing and runs without hesitation or interruption.

    • Intel® Smart Response Technology

      Intel® Smart Response Technology allows a fast SSD to be used together with an HDD, enabling faster access to frequently used files and applications.

    • Intel® Rapid Start Technology

      Intel® Rapid Start Technology allows you to quickly return to where you left off with an Ultrabook™ that goes from deep-sleep to fully awake in a flash.*
      *Requires a select Intel® processor, Intel® software and BIOS update, and Intel® Solid-State Drive (SSD). Depending on system configuration, your results may vary. Contact your system manufacturer for more information.
